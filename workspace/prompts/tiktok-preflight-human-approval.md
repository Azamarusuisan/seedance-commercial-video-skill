# TikTok Preflight Human Approval

Before upload/publish, a human owner must confirm:

## Account / Delivery Actions

- AI did not publish/send ads.
- AI did not create campaigns.
- AI did not change budget, bid, schedule, targeting, billing, account permissions, or payment method.
- AI did not delete campaigns/ad groups/ads.
- Spark Ads usage authorization is confirmed if used.
- Outside posting/comment actions are approved by a human.

## Creative

- Product name, price, discount, deadline, and conditions match LP.
- LP first view answers the ad promise.
- Proof/No.1/reviews/rankings/awards/expert claims have evidence.
- Generated people do not present fake real testimonials.
- AIGC/AI voice/avatar disclosure need is checked.
- Face/logo/music/facility/post/review/comment/screenshot rights are checked.
- Subtitles, voice, on-screen text, and CTA have no NG expressions.
- Important text/logos are inside safe zones.
- CTA is one clear action.

## Tracking / LP

- Pixel / Events API / standard events are checked.
- UTM is set.
- Mobile LP displays correctly.
- Form/purchase/reservation path works.
- Privacy policy, terms, and required disclaimers exist.
