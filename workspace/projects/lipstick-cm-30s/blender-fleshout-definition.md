# Blender Flesh-Out Definition

Blender flesh-outとは、Blenderの3Dプリビズを最終映像の絵として使うことではない。
Blenderから構造情報だけを抽出し、画像生成で写実CM品質へ変換する工程である。

## Blenderから継承するもの

- camera angle
- lens feel
- framing
- product silhouette
- product scale
- object placement
- spatial relationship
- shot continuity
- rough motion intention
- negative space
- foreground / background relationship

## Blenderから継承しないもの

- low-poly geometry
- viewport lighting
- temporary material
- gray background
- plastic shader
- flat color
- cheap CG preview look
- Blender particles / drawn rings / fake VFX lines
- placeholder planes
- any visible blockout look

## GPT Image / Higgsfield画像生成で足すもの

- photoreal material
- luxury product photography
- realistic black lacquer
- champagne-gold metal reflections
- deep rouge lipstick wax texture
- skin/lip realism when needed
- soft glints
- photographic bokeh
- volumetric haze
- rim light
- cinematic depth of field
- high-brand commercial color grade

## Seedanceに任せるもの

- camera motion
- object movement
- timing
- continuity
- subtle parallax
- transition between approved key visuals

## Seedanceに任せないもの

- exact Japanese text
- subtitles
- narration
- CTA
- logo placement
- legal claims
- product name text
- small readable text
- final audio mix
