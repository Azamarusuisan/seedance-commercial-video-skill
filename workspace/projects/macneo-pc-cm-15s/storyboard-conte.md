## What The Storyboard Image Should Look Like

- Four panels in one 16:9 sheet.
- Commercial production storyboard style.
- Clear product positions and camera movement.
- Camera arrows and motion guides are acceptable.

- Exact Japanese subtitles are overlaid later in UI/Palmier/post.

## What The Photoreal Key Visuals Should Look Like

- One clean product shot per image.
- No panel grid.

- Premium white studio, colorful laptop materials, controlled electric glints.
- Suitable as Seedance primary visual only after human approval.
