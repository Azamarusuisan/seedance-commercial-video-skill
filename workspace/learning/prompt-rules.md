# Prompt Rules

## Blender Previs Usage

Use Blender only as a composition and camera guide.
Preserve framing, object placement, scale, silhouette, negative space, and motion intent.
Do not preserve low-poly geometry, viewport lighting, flat materials, gray background, plastic shaders, placeholder planes, or cheap CG render look.

## Photoreal Language

Prefer:

- photographic bokeh
- soft glints
- volumetric haze
- rim light
- shallow depth of field
- lens bloom
- realistic reflections

Avoid:

- rings
- lines
- dots
- particles
- graphic overlays
- UI-like VFX

## Human / Cast References

Do not collage unrelated references.
Use separate references with explicit roles, or split the clip into separate shots.
For Rina Hayun-like cast usage, use lips only as anonymous lips/skin-tone reference.
No full face, no testimonial, no influencer/customer framing.

## Text Policy

No exact subtitles, CTA, product claims, small logos, or Japanese copy inside Seedance.
Add these in post-production.

## Demand Pattern

Create storyboard panels or a 15s beat sheet before generation.
Prefer `4 hooks x 1 mode` over `1 hook x 4 modes` when exploring short video demand.
